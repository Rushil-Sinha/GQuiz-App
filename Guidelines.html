How to play?

The quiz consists of questions carefully designed to help you self-assess your comprehension of the information presented on the topics selected.

  • Each Round consists of 10 questions.
  • Each Question has to be answered to move to the next question. However you can skip a question one time only. Skipping a question won't cost you any points.
  • You'll get a total of 30 secs to answer questions on your selected topic.
  • For every correct answer, you'll score 3 points.
  • For every wrong answer, 2 points will be deducted from the total score.
  • At the end of each round, you can see your scores.
